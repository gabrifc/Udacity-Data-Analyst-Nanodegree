BMI, Cholesterol, GDP(PPP) and Cancer Datasets

Description

The datasets contain information for the specified parameters from different world countries, downloaded from the Gapminder website (see http://www.gapminder.org/data/)

Details
- BMI Data
Estimated BMI from the different countries in the 1980 to 2008 period.

- Cholesterol
Estimated mmol/L Total Cholesterol in blood from different countries from the period 1980 to 2008.

- GDP (PPP)
GDP(PPP) estimate of the different countries for the period 1980 to 2008.

- Cancer Datasets
Male and female cancer incidence of different types of cancer per 100,000 habitants at different countries for the year 2002.
